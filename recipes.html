Authentic Recipes

Chicken Pelau

The national dish of Trinidad and Tobago. A savory one-pot meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken pieces
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup pigeon peas
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

Callaloo

A creamy, spicy side dish made from dasheen bush (taro leaves).

Ingredients:

  • 1 bundle dasheen bush
  • 1 cup ochroes (okra)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 crab (optional)
  • Pumpkin, onions, garlic

Curry Chicken

Rich, spicy, and perfect with roti or white rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 4 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • Hot pepper to taste
